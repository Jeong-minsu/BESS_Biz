Rationale: 2026-05-27 Yes Energy data shows GR_WEST at 627-652 MW across HE10-13 (vs 2026-05-26 where the trough was primarily concentrated at HE10-11). The broader GR_WEST trough window with 628 MW minimum at HE11 and 632 MW at HE12 creates a wider WEST_TO_NORTH signal that extends through HE13. Also, ERCOT total wind at 3,473 MW trough at HE13 was absent from the 2026-05-26 profile.

Key upgrade: HE11 and HE12 sized at HIGH 50 MW (vs 25 MW HIGH for HE10-11 in 2026-05-26 cycle). Now that Tuesday's GR_WEST trough matches Sunday's 600 MW magnitude, the full HIGH 50 MW is justified without the Sunday liquidity haircut.

Lesson embedded: When GR_WEST trough is below 650 MW across multiple consecutive hours AND ERCOT total wind confirms a double-trough (wind + solar simultaneously depressed), HIGH 50 MW is appropriate. 2026-05-26 cycle only captured two of those hours; 2026-05-27 captures four.

The 2026-05-26 cycle's HE21 position had the most structural risk (P(DA<RT) 51.9% flag, DA $53.57 extreme). ### 4.3 HE22 HIGH 50 MW Confirmed as Standard Tier for Normal Weekdays
