3. **WEST_TO_NORTH constraint attenuation**: GR_WEST wind at 2,749-3,089 MW trough is materially higher than 2026-05-24's 600 MW. The midday Long DA thesis is weaker today. If GR_WEST wind is even higher than the 2,749 MW minimum (e.g., Enverus STPF shows 3,873 MW at HE10), West export constraint may not bind at all. The cap out on Memorial Day may be somewhat lower (planned maintenance opportunistically scheduled on holiday) but this is unverifiable.

5. **No Smartbidder P(DA>RT) data**: All P estimates are reconstructed, not from calibrated Smartbidder probability model. Model uncertainty is elevated. The 0.80 confidence discount applied to risk-adjusted EV reflects this. If Smartbidder data becomes available before 10:00 CT DAM cutoff, update P estimates and re-evaluate sizing.

6. **Monday DAM efficiency vs Sunday**: Sunday DAM structural overprice edge (documented in prior learnings) does not apply Monday. Monday markets are more liquid, more efficiently clearing, reducing the systematic DA-overpricing bias that supports Short DA. P estimates are conservatively adjusted down 5-6pp from prior Sunday cycle values.
