Wind recovery from GR_WEST begins after HE15 (4,602 MW → 7,212 MW by HE18 → 9,985 MW by HE21), but this rebuild is gradual and insufficient to prevent the HE21 spike given the gross load level.

- **AS scarcity structure in HE21-23 points to reserve tightness**: The simultaneous elevation of Non-Spin ($26), RegUp ($16), and RRS ($14) at HE21 signals Smartbidder's model sees reserve margins approaching scarcity pricing thresholds in that window. With 14,639 MW offline at HE21 and net load at 54,868 MW, effective operating reserve is narrow. BESS energy dispatch plus AS stacking in HE20-22 is the highest-value window of the day.
