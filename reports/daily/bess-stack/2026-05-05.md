- **Mitigation**: file an opportunistic RT energy offer at HE19 strike $50/MWh (above DA forecast $31, below scarcity print $80+). If RT clears < $50, no fill — no loss. If RT clears > $50, dispatch and capture spike. Risk accepted: $2,000-5,000 RT upside vs ~$0 DA opportunity cost.
