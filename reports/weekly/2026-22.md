## 2. Market Structural Update (Week 2026-22)

**Source**: `reports/weekly/structural-update/2026-22.md` (market-analyst, generated 2026-06-01 CT)

### Key Policy/Rule Changes

**PGRR145 / NPRR1325 — Batch Zero Board Vote: June 1, 2026**
- ERCOT Board of Directors scheduled to vote today. Replaces individual Large Load Interconnection Study process with a system-wide batch-based approach for new large loads (data centers, crypto miners, industrial).
- If approved, effective date July 10, 2026. Rejection or material amendment resets the governance timeline by months.
- Trading implication: Locks the framework governing which large loads reach COD in 2027-2029; shapes long-run net load and scarcity price outlook.

**NPRR1309 / NOGRR283 — DRRS (Dispatchable Reliability Reserve Service)**
- TAC approved April 2026. Requires resources to run at least 4 hours at HSL, come online within 2 hours. GKS (100MW/200MWh = 2h duration) is at the margin for DRRS eligibility.
- Estimated at $5-15/MWh in early modeling. 4h+ BESS assets have structural revenue stack advantage.

**NOGRR272 AGS Incentive Program — Formally Established ~May 26, 2026**
- $1,500/MW GFM retrofit incentive for legacy ESR assets with SGIAs executed before April 1, 2026. $25 million total cap, 18-month implementation window, order-of-receipt basis.
- GKS eligible: $1,500/MW x 100 MW = **$150,000** potential incentive value. Management should evaluate GFM retrofit cost vs. this incentive plus potential future DRRS/AGS revenue advantages.
